Conclusion¶

The visualization indicate that having a larger number of tweets does not translate to high amount of retweets or favorite count. Both tweets and retweets originated from Twitter for iPhone, which must have been because ot the ease of tweeting and retweeting from the phone. There is also a very poor correlation between rating and favorite count or retweets. High ratings for a particular dog does not translate to high retweets or favorite count. This means there might be possiblly be more factors aside rating which are considered by individuals who visit the WeRateDogs twitter account.¶
